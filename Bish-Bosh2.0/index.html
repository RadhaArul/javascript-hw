Bish-Bosh

Bish Bosh is a group word game for children to teach them about division. Players take turns to count incrementally, replacing any number divisible by (for example) 3 with the word "Bish", and any number divisible by 5 with the word "Bosh" and any number divisible by both 3 and 5 with the word "Bish-Bosh".
